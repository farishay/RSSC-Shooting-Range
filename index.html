Our Story

Where Precision
Meets Passion

Located just 1KM past Karachi's Toll Plaza on Super Highway, RSSC is the only facility of its kind in Pakistan open to civilians, ladies and children. Dedicated to providing a secure and enjoyable indoor/outdoor environment for shooters of all ability levels.

From Olympic-standard Skeet & Trap to a 1000-yard rifle range and big bore up to 600M — RSSC ranges are fully capable of hosting prestigious international shooting competitions by day or by night.
